Parent Blogger Club — UK Parenting Blogs

A historical ranking of the UK's most influential parenting bloggers, originally published on rise.global. Scored on Kred Influence, Kred Outreach, blog posts, and blog comments for the fortnight ending 17 December 2018.

Score period: 10 Dec – 17 Dec 2018 Released: 17 December 2018 59 bloggers archived

This page is a preserved archive of the Parent Blogger Club, a community leaderboard that ran on rise.global ranking the UK's most influential parenting bloggers each fortnight. The list celebrated mum bloggers, dad bloggers, and family lifestyle creators across travel, food, education, and home life.

The 17 December 2018 release captures the community at its peak — Over 40 And A Mum To One holding the top spot, Jenny TheBrickCastle and Zena's Suitcase rounding out the top three, and dozens more familiar names from the UK parenting blog scene. Many of these creators went on to build full-fledged content brands, podcasts, and media careers. We are keeping it online because the original Kiddy Charts and All The Beautiful Things write-ups, plus countless personal blog posts, still cite it.

About the methodology

The Parent Blogger Club was a dynamic leaderboard, recomputed every two weeks. Each blogger received a Blogger Score blending four signals: Kred Influence (the likelihood that other accounts re-share or engage with their content), Kred Outreach (the breadth of accounts they actively engaged in return), blog posts published in the period, and blog comments received.

The "Move" column shows the change in rank versus the previous fortnight — a snapshot of momentum in a tight-knit community.

Note: This is a static archive of the 59 entries from the 17 December 2018 release. The data is no longer updated. Some bloggers listed have since rebranded, gone dormant, or moved to other platforms; the descriptions reflect the bloggers' own Twitter bios at the time.
