#travel1k — The Top 1000+ Travel Bloggers

A historical ranking of the world's most influential travel bloggers, originally published on rise.global. Scored on Kred Influence and Outreach signals for the week ending 30 May 2018.

Score period: 23 – 30 May 2018 Released: 30 May 2018 Top 298 entries archived

This page is a preserved archive of #travel1k — The Top 1000+ Travel Bloggers, a leaderboard that ran on rise.global through 2018. It tracked the most influential voices in travel blogging each week, blending Kred Influence (reach) and Kred Outreach (engagement) into a single travel1k score.

The list captures the golden age of independent travel blogging — Matt Matt holding the #1 spot, Charles McCool of McCool Travel and Rick Griffin in the top three, plus hundreds of family travel, luxury travel, food travel, and adventure travel bloggers shaping the industry. Many of these creators went on to become full-fledged media brands; others became household names in travel media. We are keeping it online because press articles, blogger sites, and travel industry round-ups still cite it.

About the methodology

#travel1k was a dynamic leaderboard, recomputed weekly on rise.global. Each blogger received a travel1k score derived from Kred — a social authority metric that measured Influence (the likelihood that other accounts re-share, reply to, or engage with you) and Outreach (the breadth of accounts you actively engage in return). The two were blended to surface bloggers who were both reach-strong and genuinely interactive.

The "Move" column shows the change in rank versus the previous week — a snapshot of momentum in a fast-moving creator economy.

Note: This is a static archive of the top 298 entries from the 30 May 2018 release. The data is no longer updated. Some bloggers listed have since rebranded, gone dormant, or moved to other platforms; the descriptions reflect the bloggers' own Twitter bios at the time.
