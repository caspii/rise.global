The Accounting Top 100

A historical ranking of the 100 most influential people in accounting on social media, originally published on rise.global and sponsored by Avalara. Score period 15 – 29 March 2023.

Score period: 15 Mar – 29 Mar 2023 Released: 29 March 2023 Top 100 entries archived

This page is a preserved archive of The Accounting Top 100, a social media leaderboard that ran on rise.global, ranking the 100 most influential CPAs, tax professionals, accounting firms, and educators by their reach and engagement online.

The leaderboard was originally sponsored by Avalara, the tax compliance software company, and frequently cited by Accounting Today, CPA Practice Advisor, and the broader accounting press. The 29 March 2023 release captures a moment when the profession was actively wrestling with the talent crisis, the rise of AI in tax, and the ongoing modernization of firm management — themes reflected throughout the rankings. We are keeping it online because press articles, firm websites, and industry round-ups still link to it.

About the methodology

The Accounting Top 100 was recomputed every two weeks. Each person received a Power Score based on their social media activity and engagement during the score period, with weighting designed to favour authentic professional conversation over raw follower counts.

The "Move" column shows the change in rank versus the previous bulletin — useful for spotting rising voices in the profession.

Note: This is a static archive of the 29 March 2023 release. The data is no longer updated. The descriptions reflect each person's own Twitter bio at the time.
