The 100 Most Influential Blockchain Companies

A historical ranking of the 100 most influential blockchain companies, originally published on rise.global by Richtopia. Scored on Klout and Twitter signals for the week ending 9 May 2018.

Published by Richtopia Score period: 2 May – 9 May 2018 Released: 9 May 2018

This page is a preserved archive of the Blockchain 100 leaderboard that ran on rise.global between 2017 and 2018. It tracked the 100 most influential blockchain companies on social media each week, blending Klout authority scores with Twitter list inclusion to surface the projects shaping the conversation.

The leaderboard captured a remarkable moment in crypto history — Ethereum reclaiming the top spot from Coinbase, Kraken jumping 13 places, Pantera Capital climbing 11, and now-defunct exchanges like Coincheck still in the mix. Names that would later become household: Binance at #11, Cardano at #28, Tezos at #59, IOTA at #88. We are keeping it online because too many press articles, blogs, and academic sources still cite it.

About the methodology

The Blockchain 100 was a dynamic leaderboard, recomputed weekly and published by Richtopia on rise.global. Each company was given a Power Score derived from Klout (a now-retired social authority metric) and the count of Twitter lists the account appeared on, weighted to balance reach with curated relevance.

The "Move" column shows the change in rank versus the previous week — a snapshot of momentum within an industry that, in 2018, was moving very fast.

Note: This is a static archive. The data is frozen at the 9 May 2018 release and is no longer updated. Some companies listed have since rebranded, merged, or shut down; the descriptions reflect the companies' own Twitter bios at the time.
